About Us

At Madhvi’s Makeover Studio, we proudly serve JNPT Township, Dronagiri, Uran, and clients across Navi Mumbai with expert beauty and hair care services. Founded by Lakmé Academy alumna Mrs. Madhvi, our studio blends artistic skill with professional training to bring out the best in every client.

We specialize in bridal makeup, hair styling, skin treatments, facials, party looks, saree draping, and more—tailored for every occasion. With a growing reputation as one of the most trusted salons in the area, we are committed to making every client look and feel their absolute best.
